Team members can actively engage in real-time,
making the development of user stories and acceptance criteria a collective effort.
This real-time interaction reduces misunderstandings and ensures that everyone is on the same page.

By allowing multiple users to contribute simultaneously,
the feature significantly speeds up the process of defining
and refining user stories and acceptance criteria.
This leads to a more efficient workflow,
as teams can quickly iterate and agree on project requirements.
